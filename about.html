About Me

My Story

Hi, I’m Jeff. I’m a UI Designer with a background in Technology. Before my transition into UI Design, I worked closely with a corporate client to maintain and develop websites and content to their requirements.

After some research, I came across UI design and found similar principles that were applied in Front End design. I wanted to focus on identifying the client’s wants and requirements in the tech field, so this switch felt natural to me. Incorporating both my empathy and creativity, I want to develop satisfying solutions and Designs.

I decided to enrol in a UI immersion course with CareerFoundry so I would be better prepared for a product design role. There, I gained knowledge in user experience research and design, user interface design.

When I am not designing, I enjoy coding for the retro C64, reading, and enjoying quality time with my dog Olive!
